Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology

Premier Private University Education in Bhubaneswar, India

About the University

Established in 1992 by Dr. Achyuta Samanta, Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is a premier private deemed university. Serving over 40,000 students, KIIT is accredited by NAAC (A++ grade), NBA, and approved by UGC and AICTE. Known for its academic excellence and social initiatives, it offers world-class education across multiple disciplines.

Academic Programs

KIIT offers over 20 undergraduate B.Tech programs (4 years), including Computer Science, Electronics, Civil, and Biotechnology. It also provides postgraduate programs (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) and PhD degrees. Admissions are through KIITEE scores, with options for JEE Main or management quota. The curriculum emphasizes research, innovation, and industry-oriented training, with international exchange programs.

Campus & Facilities

KIIT’s 400-acre campus in Bhubaneswar features modern classrooms, advanced labs, a central library, and hostels for over 20,000 students. Facilities include a 500-bed hospital, sports complexes (cricket, swimming, tennis), and innovation centers. The campus hosts a supercomputer and supports research through 30 dedicated centers, fostering a vibrant academic environment.

Rankings & Accreditation

KIIT is ranked 16th among universities and 20th in engineering by NIRF 2024, and 151–200 in Asia by QS World University Rankings 2025. It holds NAAC A++ accreditation, NBA, and ABET accreditation for select programs. Recognized by UGC and AICTE, KIIT has strong industry partnerships and a 95% placement rate.

Student Life

With over 40,000 students, KIIT hosts vibrant events like KIIT Fest (cultural) and Innovate (technical). Clubs like Qutopia (quizzing), robotics, and sports teams (cricket, basketball) foster engagement. The campus offers scholarships, international exchange programs, and a ragging-free environment, promoting holistic development.
