About Karshi State University

Established in 1992, Karshi State University (KSU) is a leading institution in Uzbekistan, located in the vibrant city of Karshi. Recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Medical Commission (NMC), KSU is a top destination for international students seeking affordable, high-quality medical education.

KSU offers a 6-year MBBS program (5 years academic + 1-year internship) taught in English, supported by modern facilities, including advanced laboratories and affiliated hospitals for clinical training. With a diverse student community and experienced faculty, KSU provides a strong foundation for a global medical career.

  • WHO & NMC Recognized
  • English-Medium MBBS Program
  • Affordable Tuition & Indian Mess
  • Modern Labs & Clinical Training
