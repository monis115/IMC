Galgotias University

Leading Private University Education in Greater Noida, India

About the University

Established in 2011, Galgotias University in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, is a private university recognized by UGC and AICTE. Serving over 30,000 students, it is accredited by NAAC (A+ grade) and known for its industry-aligned curriculum and global partnerships with institutions like Purdue University, USA.

Academic Programs

Galgotias University offers over 20 B.Tech programs (4 years), including Computer Science, AI, Cyber Security, and Mechanical Engineering, alongside MBA, BBA, and PhD programs. Admissions are through GUEE, JEE Main, or merit-based criteria. The university emphasizes hands-on learning, with international exchange programs and industry certifications.

Campus & Facilities

The 52-acre campus in Greater Noida features modern labs, a central library, Wi-Fi-enabled classrooms, and hostels for over 10,000 students. Facilities include sports complexes (cricket, football, volleyball), a cafeteria, and an incubation center for startups, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.

Rankings & Accreditation

Ranked 151–200 among universities by NIRF 2024 and accredited by NAAC (A+ grade). Galgotias University is recognized by UGC and AICTE, with a 98% placement rate, attracting recruiters like Infosys, TCS, and Amazon.

Student Life

With over 30,000 students, Galgotias hosts events like G-Quasar (cultural fest) and tech fests. Clubs for coding, robotics, and sports (cricket, basketball) foster engagement. Scholarships, international tie-ups, and a vibrant campus life enhance student experiences.
