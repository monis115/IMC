About Andijan State Medical Institute

Founded in 1955, Andijan State Medical Institute (ASMI) is one of the leading medical universities in Uzbekistan, located in the city of Andijan. The institute is recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO), National Medical Commission (NMC), and other international medical bodies.

ASMI offers a 6-year MBBS program taught in English, making it an attractive destination for international students. With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and affordable education, it provides a strong foundation for a medical career.

  • Recognized by WHO & NMC
  • English Medium Instruction
  • Affordable Tuition Fees
  • Modern Infrastructure
