About Urgench State Medical Institute

Established in 1992, Urgench State Medical Institute is a premier medical institution located in Urgench City, Uzbekistan. Recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Medical Commission (NMC), it is a preferred destination for international students seeking affordable, high-quality medical education.

The institute offers a 6-year MBBS program (5 years academic + 1-year internship) taught in English, supported by state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced laboratories, clinical training centers, and well-equipped hostels. With a focus on practical training and a diverse student community, Urgench State Medical Institute prepares students for global medical careers.

  • WHO & NMC Recognized
  • English-Medium MBBS Program
  • Affordable Tuition & Indian Mess
  • Modern Labs & Clinical Training
