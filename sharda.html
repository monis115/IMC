Sharda University

Excellence in Engineering Education since 2009

About the University

Established in 2009, Sharda University is a premier private university located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. The university is recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE, offering world-class education across multiple disciplines. With a sprawling campus spread over 63 acres, Sharda University has emerged as one of the leading educational institutions in the Delhi-NCR region, known for its innovative teaching methods and industry-focused curriculum.

Academic Programs

Sharda University offers comprehensive B.Tech programs (4 years) across multiple specializations including Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics & Communication, and Electrical Engineering. The university also provides integrated programs like B.Tech + MBA and specialized courses in emerging fields like Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Admissions are based on JEE Main scores, university entrance tests, or management quota. The curriculum emphasizes practical learning with industry internships and live projects.

Campus & Facilities

The 63-acre campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, a well-stocked library, and advanced research facilities. The university provides excellent hostel accommodation for over 8,000 students with separate facilities for boys and girls. Campus amenities include sports complexes, gymnasiums, swimming pools, medical facilities, cafeterias, and recreation centers. The campus also houses incubation centers and innovation labs to foster entrepreneurship and research.

Financial Details

B.Tech tuition fees range from ₹2.5 Lakh to ₹4 Lakh per year depending on specialization. Hostel fees range from ₹1.2 Lakh to ₹1.75 Lakh per year for different accommodation types.
For detailed fee structure and latest updates, please contact us directly.

Rankings & Accreditation

Sharda University is recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE with strong accreditation credentials. The university has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in North India. It maintains partnerships with international universities and has received recognition for its research initiatives and academic excellence. The university's placement record reflects its industry standing with a consistent placement rate of over 85% for engineering graduates.

Student Life

With over 25,000 students from 50+ countries, Sharda University offers a vibrant multicultural environment. The campus hosts numerous technical and cultural festivals, including the annual Sharda University Cultural Festival and technical symposiums. Student clubs and societies cover areas like robotics, coding, literature, arts, and sports. The university has excellent sports facilities and regularly participates in inter-university competitions. International exchange programs and study abroad opportunities enhance the global learning experience.
