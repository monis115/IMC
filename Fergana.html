About Fergana Medical Institute of Public Health

Established in 1991 under Fergana State University, the Fergana Medical Institute of Public Health (FMIPH) is a premier medical institution in Uzbekistan, located in the vibrant city of Fergana. Recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the National Medical Commission (NMC, formerly MCI), FMIPH is a top choice for international students, particularly from India, seeking quality medical education at affordable costs.

The institute offers a 6-year MBBS program (5 years academic + 1-year internship) taught in English, featuring state-of-the-art facilities like 3D simulation labs and a well-stocked library with Indian study materials. With over 350 Indian students currently enrolled and a faculty of 147 professors across 19 departments, FMIPH provides a robust academic environment, blending theoretical and practical training.
