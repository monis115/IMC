Karaganda State Medical University

Excellence in Medical Education in Karaganda, Kazakhstan

About the University

Established in 1950, Karaganda State Medical University (KSMU) is one of Kazakhstan’s oldest and most respected medical institutions, located in the dynamic city of Karaganda. Recognized by WHO, NMC (India), UNESCO, and the Ministry of Education and Science of Kazakhstan, KSMU offers a globally accredited MBBS program in English, attracting over 8,000 students, including a large international community.

Academic Programs

KSMU offers a 5-year MBBS program in English (plus a 1-year internship), alongside programs in nursing, dentistry, pharmacy, and public health. The curriculum focuses on hands-on training in state-of-the-art labs and hospitals, preparing students for global medical careers. Indian students must qualify for NEET, with no additional entrance exam required, ensuring accessible high-quality education.

Campus & Facilities

KSMU’s campus in Karaganda features modern infrastructure, including advanced laboratories, a vast library, and clinical training centers. Secure hostels provide Wi-Fi, laundry, and Indian mess facilities, catering to international students. Karaganda’s vibrant urban environment offers access to cultural sites, dining, and recreational opportunities, enriching student life.

Rankings & Accreditation

KSMU is ranked ~5000 globally (4ICU) with a national ranking of 12 in Kazakhstan. Accredited by WHO, NMC, and UNESCO, its MBBS degree is recognized worldwide, enabling graduates to practice in India after passing the FMGE. KSMU’s long-standing commitment to excellence has solidified its reputation in medical education.

Student Life

With over 8,000 students, KSMU fosters a vibrant campus community, including many Indian students. Cultural festivals, sports, and student clubs create a lively atmosphere. The Indian mess offers familiar cuisine, while Karaganda’s amenities—malls, restaurants, and parks—provide a balanced lifestyle. KSMU supports international students with visa assistance and career counseling.
