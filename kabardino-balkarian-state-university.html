KBSU MEDICAL

Kabardino-Balkarian State University

Nalchik, Russia

Overview

Kabardino-Balkarian State University (KBSU) is one of the leading classical universities in the North Caucasus region. Located in Nalchik, it offers a wide range of academic programs, with its medical faculty being highly regarded for its structured curriculum, modern facilities, generic international environment. It is known for providing high-quality medical education at an affordable cost.

History & Legacy

  • Founded in 1957, KBSU has a long history of academic excellence.
  • One of the largest universities in the North Caucasus region.
  • Consistently ranked among the top classical universities in Russia.

Recognition & Accreditation

  • Accredited by the Ministry of Science and Higher Education of the Russian Federation.
  • Listed in the World Directory of Medical Schools (WDOMS).
  • Recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) of India and WHO.

Academic Programs

MBBS Course: 6-year program structured to meet international standards.

Medium: English and Russian.

Focus: Comprehensive medical training, clinical skills, and research.

Campus, Facilities & Student Life

  • Located in the scenic city of Nalchik, known for its resorts and natural beauty.
  • Modern campus with libraries, sports complexes, and dormitories.
  • Vibrant student life with various cultural and sports activities.

Admission & Eligibility

  • NEET qualification required for Indian students.
  • High school diploma with good grades in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Direct admission process available for international students.

Fees & Ranking

KBSU offers very affordable tuition fees compared to other Russian universities, making it an attractive option for international students.

One Time Charge :- 1000$ - 1500$
