Asfendiyarov Kazakh National Medical University

Premier Medical Education in Almaty, Kazakhstan

About the University

Founded in 1930, Asfendiyarov Kazakh National Medical University (KazNMU) is Kazakhstan’s leading medical institution, located in Almaty. Granted national university status in 2001, it is recognized by WHO, NMC (India), UNESCO, and the Ministry of Education and Science of Kazakhstan. With over 11,000 students and 1,500+ specialized faculty, KazNMU has trained over 80,000 doctors, boasting a strong academic legacy.

Academic Programs

KazNMU offers a 5-year MBBS program in English with a 1-year mandatory internship, alongside degrees in nursing, dentistry, pharmacy, and public health. The curriculum balances theoretical and practical training in modern labs and affiliated hospitals. Indian students require NEET qualification, with no entrance exam, ensuring accessible, high-quality education aligned with global standards.

Campus & Facilities

Located in Almaty, KazNMU’s campus features advanced infrastructure, including libraries, computer labs, and clinical training centers. Hostels accommodate over 2,000 students with Wi-Fi, kitchens, and Indian mess facilities. Almaty’s vibrant urban setting offers cultural landmarks, dining, and recreational activities, enhancing the student experience.

Rankings & Accreditation

KazNMU ranks 5th in Kazakhstan and ~3878 globally (4ICU). Accredited by WHO, NMC, and UNESCO, its MBBS degree is globally recognized, allowing graduates to practice in India after passing the FMGE. KazNMU’s high FMGE scores and academic discipline make it a top choice for medical education.

Student Life

KazNMU’s campus hosts over 11,000 students, including many from India, fostering a vibrant community through cultural festivals, sports, and clubs like the Debate Club and Volunteer Club. Indian mess provides familiar cuisine, while Almaty’s amenities—malls, restaurants, and parks—offer a balanced lifestyle. Visa support and career guidance are provided.
