Al-Farabi Kazakh National University

Excellence in Education and Research | Almaty, Kazakhstan

About the University

Established in 1934, Al-Farabi Kazakh National University (KazNU) is Kazakhstan’s premier institution, named after the renowned philosopher Al-Farabi. Located in Almaty, it is a hub for academic excellence, research, and innovation. KazNU ranks 150th in the QS World University Rankings 2025 and leads Kazakhstan’s higher education landscape. As a member of the UN Academic Impact Program, it has driven global initiatives like the Global Hub on Sustainable Development. With over 25,000 students and 4,000 faculty, KazNU offers a dynamic and inclusive environment.

Academic Programs

KazNU offers 134 Bachelor’s, 268 Master’s, and 140 PhD programs across 17 faculties, including medicine, engineering, sciences, humanities, and business. Its MBBS program, taught in English, is globally recognized, attracting international students. The university emphasizes research-driven education, with state-of-the-art labs and partnerships with global institutions, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for their careers.

Campus & Facilities

The 100-hectare Kazgugrad campus in Almaty is a vibrant academic city, featuring 13 academic buildings, 17 dormitories, and advanced research facilities like the Farabi Chem Science cluster. The iconic 15-story main building houses key faculties, while a second campus supports specialized departments. Students enjoy modern libraries, sports complexes, and cultural centers, all set against the scenic Alatau mountains.

Rankings & Achievements

KazNU is ranked 150th globally (QS World University Rankings 2025) and 44th in QS Asia Rankings. It has received the Presidential Award for Quality, the CIS Award for quality and services, and the 2020 Global Islamic Finance Award. As a leader in the UN Model New Silk Road program, KazNU is a globally recognized institution committed to sustainable development.

Student Life

With 25,000+ students, including 2,500 international students, KazNU offers a vibrant community. The campus hosts cultural festivals, sports events, and student organizations. Almaty’s urban amenities, combined with the campus’s scenic location, provide an ideal balance of study and leisure. Modern dormitories and dining options cater to diverse needs, fostering a global perspective.
