RV College of Engineering

Premier Engineering Education in Bangalore, India

About the University

Established in 1963, RV College of Engineering (RVCE) in Bangalore, Karnataka, is a leading autonomous private engineering college managed by Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST). Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum, and accredited by AICTE and NBA, RVCE is one of India’s top self-financing engineering institutions. Located 13 km from Bangalore’s city center, it serves over 1,400 students annually.

Academic Programs

RVCE offers 15 undergraduate Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) programs, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science, and Aerospace Engineering, all with a duration of 4 years. It also provides 14 Master’s programs (M.Tech, MCA, M.Arch) and Doctoral studies. All courses are taught in English and hold autonomous status from VTU. Admission is through Karnataka CET, COMEDK UGET, or management quota.

Campus & Facilities

Spread across 16.85 acres on Mysore Road, RVCE’s campus features smart classrooms, an auditorium, conference halls, a cafeteria, gym, and separate hostels for boys and girls. The college has modern labs, a library, and facilities for sports and extracurricular activities, including teams like Team Helios Racing and Team Antariksh, fostering innovation and student engagement.

For detailed fee structure and latest updates, please contact us directly.

Rankings & Accreditation

RVCE is ranked 99th among engineering colleges in India by NIRF 2024 and is among the top 10 self-financing engineering institutions. It is accredited by AICTE, NBA, and affiliated with VTU. The college’s Mechanical Engineering program was the first in Karnataka’s private sector to receive NBA accreditation. RVCE’s degrees are recognized globally for industry and research opportunities.

Student Life

RVCE fosters a vibrant student community with over 1,400 students participating in technical and cultural events like 8th Mile, the annual techno-cultural fest. Clubs like IEEE, ACM, and robotics teams (e.g., Team Antariksh) encourage innovation. The campus offers scholarships, sports facilities, and a ragging-free environment, making it ideal for holistic development.
