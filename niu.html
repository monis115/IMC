Noida International University

Leading Innovation in Higher Education since 2010

About the University

Established in 2010, Noida International University (NIU) is a prestigious private university located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. NIU has achieved remarkable distinction by securing the highest score of 3.44 out of 4 with Grade A+ in its first NAAC accreditation cycle, outperforming all other private universities in India. The university is recognized by UGC, approved by AICTE, and is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU), offering world-class education across diverse disciplines.

Academic Programs

NIU offers comprehensive B.Tech programs (4 years) through its School of Engineering and Technology, covering specializations in Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics & Communication, and emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. The university provides 12 schools offering diverse programs from undergraduate to doctoral levels. Admissions are based on JEE Main scores, university entrance examinations, or merit-based selection. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and research-oriented education.

Campus & Facilities

NIU's modern campus spans over 75 acres and features state-of-the-art infrastructure including advanced laboratories, smart classrooms, well-equipped libraries, and cutting-edge research facilities. The university provides excellent residential facilities with modern hostels accommodating thousands of students. Campus amenities include sports complexes, fitness centers, medical facilities, multiple dining options, and recreational spaces. The campus also houses innovation centers, incubation hubs, and industry collaboration spaces to foster entrepreneurship and practical learning.

Financial Details

B.Tech tuition fees vary by specialization and admission category. The university offers merit-based scholarships and financial assistance programs for deserving students.
For detailed fee structure and latest updates, please contact us directly.

Rankings & Accreditation

NIU holds the prestigious NAAC A+ accreditation with the highest score of 3.44 out of 4 among all private universities in India. The university has been ranked 81st in the Overall category by IIRF in 2024 and maintains strong recognition in various national rankings. NIU's School of Engineering and Technology has achieved a remarkable 75% placement rate, while the School of Business Management recorded 81% placements in 2023-2024. The university's commitment to quality education and research excellence continues to enhance its reputation nationally and internationally.

Student Life

NIU fosters a vibrant campus life with students from diverse backgrounds and regions. The university organizes numerous technical symposiums, cultural festivals, and international conferences throughout the year. Student societies and clubs cover areas including robotics, coding, entrepreneurship, arts, literature, and sports. The university has excellent sports facilities and regularly participates in inter-university competitions. International collaborations and exchange programs provide students with global exposure and learning opportunities, making NIU a truly international educational destination.
