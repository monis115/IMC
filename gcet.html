Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology

Premier Private Engineering Education in Greater Noida, India

About the College

Established in 2000, Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology (GCET) in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, is a private engineering college affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU). Approved by AICTE and accredited by NBA, it serves over 4,000 students and is known for its strong industry connections and placement record.

Academic Programs

GCET offers B.Tech programs (4 years) in Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering, along with M.Tech and MBA programs. Admissions are through JEE Main, UPSEE, or management quota. The curriculum focuses on practical training, with industry projects and internships to enhance employability.

Campus & Facilities

The 19-acre campus features advanced labs, a central library, Wi-Fi-enabled classrooms, and hostels for over 2,000 students. Sports facilities include cricket, basketball, and badminton courts, complemented by a cafeteria and innovation labs. The college supports entrepreneurship through its incubation center.

Rankings & Accreditation

Accredited by NBA and approved by AICTE, GCET is recognized for its academic excellence and 95% placement rate. Top recruiters include Infosys, Wipro, and Cognizant, with strong industry linkages ensuring job-ready graduates.

Student Life

With over 4,000 students, GCET hosts cultural and technical fests like Innovate and G-Fest. Clubs for coding, robotics, and sports (cricket, volleyball) promote engagement. Scholarships and industry collaborations enhance student opportunities on a vibrant campus.
