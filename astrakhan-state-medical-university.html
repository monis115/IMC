ASTRAKHAN

Astrakhan State Medical University

Astrakhan, Russia

Overview

Astrakhan State Medical University (ASMU) is one of Russia’s oldest and most prestigious government medical universities. It was established in 1918 in Astrakhan, a major city on the Volga River and has a recognized legacy of medical education and research spanning more than a century.

History & Legacy

  • Established on 7 November 1918, making it one of the earliest medical universities in Russia.
  • Evolved over decades into a comprehensive centre for medical, dental and pharmacy education.

Recognition & Accreditation

  • Officially recognized by WHO, NMC (India), and other global bodies.
  • Degrees are accepted internationally, enabling graduates to pursue careers or further training around the world.

Academic Programs

MBBS: Offers a 6-year MBBS program (5 academic + 1 internship).

Faculties: Runs faculties in General Medicine, Dentistry, Pharmacy and related fields.

Medium: Instruction available in English and Russian, catering to international students.

Campus & Student Life

  • Situated in central Astrakhan with well-equipped teaching hospitals and laboratories.
  • The university houses one of the largest medical libraries in the region with extensive resources for study and research.
  • Large and vibrant international student community including Indian students celebrated on campus.
  • Cultural events and supportive student services enhance global integration.

Admission & Eligibility

  • Minimum eligibility often requires 50% in Physics, Chemistry & Biology in Class 12 and NEET qualification for Indian students.
  • IELTS/TOEFL generally not required for admission into the English medium MBBS program.

Rankings & Standing

Country rank around 285th and global ranking within 6000+ range depending on ranking source. Recognized as a leader in medical and pharmaceutical education within its region.
