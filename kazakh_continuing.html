Kazakh Medical University of Continuing Education

Excellence in Medical Education in Almaty, Kazakhstan

About the University

Founded in 1963, Kazakh Medical University of Continuing Education (KMUCE) is a distinguished medical institution in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Recognized by WHO, NMC (India), and the Ministry of Education and Science of Kazakhstan, KMUCE is dedicated to scientific excellence and high-quality healthcare education. With over 11,000 students and 1,500 faculty, including 200 doctors of science, it offers a robust platform for medical training.

Academic Programs

KMUCE offers a 6-year MBBS program in English (5 years + 1-year internship), alongside courses in dentistry and nursing. The curriculum emphasizes practical skills through virtual/augmented reality training, clinical labs, and hospital exposure from the first year. Indian students require NEET qualification, with no IELTS/TOEFL needed, ensuring accessible education. The university also provides postgraduate and clinical residency programs in 28 specialties.

Campus & Facilities

Located in Almaty, KMUCE’s campus features 7 educational buildings, a practical skills center, a library with over 1 million books, and 2 anatomical museums. Facilities include virtual reality training labs, an indoor hospital for research, a canteen with Indian mess, and sports grounds. Hostels offer Wi-Fi and are near campus, fostering a supportive environment for international students.

Rankings & Accreditation

KMUCE is accredited by WHO and NMC, with its MBBS degree recognized globally, enabling graduates to practice in India after passing the FMGE (16.1% passing rate). While not ranked in QS, it holds an estimated national ranking of ~30–40 and a global ranking of ~7000–8000, reflecting its focus on affordable, quality education.

Student Life

KMUCE hosts over 11,000 students, including many from India, creating a vibrant community through cultural events, sports, and academic clubs. The Indian mess provides familiar cuisine, while Almaty’s urban amenities—malls, restaurants, and parks—enhance student life. The university offers visa support, career counseling, and a ragging-free environment, ensuring a welcoming experience.
